# Legal Preservation Policy

ARC-Emulator is built for preservation, research, interoperability, homebrew development, and legally owned software.

## ARC-Emulator does not include

- commercial ROMs
- commercial ISOs
- BIOS files
- firmware dumps
- copyrighted assets
- console keys
- decryption material
- bypass tooling for access controls

Users are responsible for ensuring that any software, firmware, BIOS, or media image used with ARC-Emulator was obtained lawfully.
